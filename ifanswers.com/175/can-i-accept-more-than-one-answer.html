No. You can only mark one answer as the accepted answer.

If no answers fully answer the question, don't mark any answer as accepted.

If the question is fully answered by a combination of answers, either edit an answer or post a new one. The idea is that people shouldn't have to scroll through multiple answers to get the solution they need.
