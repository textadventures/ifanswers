Best answer

The IFDB has lots of reviews and star ratings.

Another place you can check for reviews is IFWiki, especially if the game you're looking for was entered in a competition. The competition's page on IFWiki (for instance, the IFComp 2013 page) will often have links to reviews for the games.

You can also check the IFRO website.
