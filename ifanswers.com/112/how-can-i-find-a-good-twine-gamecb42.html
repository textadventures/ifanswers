One systematic way:

  1. Go to the IFDB.
  2. Search for games tagged 'twine'. Hope like mad that you don't get stories about string.
  3. In the ickle drop down menu at the top of your search results, select 'Highest Rated First'. 
  4. Hope that people's tastes are similar to yours, and are indicative of objective quality, whatever that is in IF.

Less systematic way: look for past year IFComp games, or other competitions, and seek out highly rated Twine games. Again, assume that your idea of 'good' is similar to other people's.
