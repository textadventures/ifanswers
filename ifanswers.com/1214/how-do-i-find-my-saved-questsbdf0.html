I have Windows 7. For me, there is a Quest folder in the Documents library. That should be the default location for Windows 7.

But I used the offline editor; I downloaded quest, I didn't make the games on textadventures.co.uk

If you still can't find your game, please tell us what operating system you are using and whether you made the game on textadventures.co.uk or locally on your computer with a downloaded Quest program.
